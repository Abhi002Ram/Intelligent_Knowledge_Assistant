AquilaAI is a B2B AI SaaS platform designed to provide enterprise-grade document intelligence using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).

The platform enables organizations to query internal knowledge bases using natural language while ensuring responses are grounded in verified source documents.

## What AquilaAI Does
- Ingests and indexes internal documents
- Converts documents into semantic representations
- Retrieves relevant information based on user queries
- Generates answers strictly grounded in retrieved content
- Provides source citations for transparency

## Core Value Proposition
AquilaAI reduces time spent searching through internal documents while minimizing hallucinations commonly observed in standalone language models.

## Intended Use
- Internal knowledge assistants
- Support and operations teams
- Sales and pre-sales engineering
- Onboarding and training support
