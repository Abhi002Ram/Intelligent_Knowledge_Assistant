# Pricing Plans

AquilaAI offers multiple pricing tiers to support organizations of different sizes and requirements.

## Free Plan
- Maximum of 50 indexed documents
- Access to community-supported language models
- Basic semantic search and Q&A
- No support for custom prompt tuning
- Community-based support only

## Pro Plan
- Maximum of 500 indexed documents
- Priority inference over Free plan
- Custom prompt templates
- Enhanced retrieval configuration options
- Email-based support

## Enterprise Plan
- Unlimited document ingestion
- Dedicated deployment options
- Advanced retrieval and indexing controls
- Custom integration support
- SLA-backed enterprise support
